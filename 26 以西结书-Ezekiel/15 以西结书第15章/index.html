结15:1
And the word of the LORD came me, saying,
耶和华的话临到我说：


结15:2
Son of man, What is the tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?
“人子啊，葡萄树比别样树有什么强处？葡萄枝比众树枝有什么好处？


结15:3
Shall wood be taken to do any work? or will men take a of it to hang any vessel ?
其上可以取木料做什么工用？可以取来做钉子挂什么器皿吗？


结15:4
, it is the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?
看哪！已经抛在火中当作柴烧，火既烧了两头，中间也被烧了，还有益于工用吗？


结15:5
, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire it, and it is burned?
完全的时候尚且不合乎什么工用，何况被火烧坏，还能合乎什么工用吗？


结15:6
Therefore thus the Lord GOD; As the tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the of .
所以主耶和华如此说：众树以内的葡萄树，我怎样使它在火中当柴，也必照样待耶路撒冷的居民。


结15:7
And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall them; and ye shall know that I am the LORD, when I set my face against them.
我必向他们变脸，他们虽从火中出来，火却要烧灭他们。我向他们变脸的时候，你们就知道我是耶和华。


结15:8

我必使地土荒凉，因为他们行事干犯我。这是主耶和华说的。”
