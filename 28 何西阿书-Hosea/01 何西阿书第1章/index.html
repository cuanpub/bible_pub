何1:1
The word of the LORD that came , the son of , in the days of , , , and , kings of , and in the days of the son of , king of Israel.
当乌西雅、约坦、亚哈斯、希西家作犹大王，约阿施的儿子耶罗波安作以色列王的时候，耶和华的话临到备利的儿子何西阿。


何1:2
The beginning of the word of the LORD by . And the LORD said to , Go, take a wife of and children of : for the land committed great , the LORD.
耶和华初次与何西阿说话，对他说：“你去娶淫妇为妻，也收那从淫乱所生的儿女；因为这地大行淫乱，离弃耶和华。”


何1:3
So he went and took the daughter of ; which conceived, and bare him a son.
于是，何西阿去娶了滴拉音的女儿歌蔑。这妇人怀孕，给他生了一个儿子。


何1:4
And the LORD said him, Call his name ; for yet a little while, and I will the blood of upon the house of , and will cause to cease the kingdom of the house of Israel.
耶和华对何西阿说：“给他起名叫耶斯列；因为再过片时，我必讨耶户家在耶斯列杀人流血的罪，也必使以色列家的国灭绝。


何1:5
And it shall come to pass at that day, that I will break the of Israel in the valley of .
到那日，我必在耶斯列平原折断以色列的弓。”


何1:6
And she conceived again, and bare a daughter. And God said him, Call her name Loruhamah: for I will no more the house of Israel; but I will take them away.
歌篾又怀孕生了一个女儿，耶和华对何西阿说：“给她起名叫罗路哈玛（就是“不蒙怜悯”的意思）；因为我必不再怜悯以色列家，决不赦免他们。


何1:7
But I will the house of , and will save them by the LORD their God, and will not save them by , nor by sword, nor by battle, by horses, nor by .
我却要怜悯犹大家，使他们靠耶和华他们的 神得救，不使他们靠弓、刀、争战、马匹与马兵得救。”


何1:8
Now when she had Loruhamah, she conceived, and bare a son.
歌篾给罗路哈玛断奶以后，又怀孕生了一个儿子。


何1:9
Then said God, Call his name : for ye are not my people, and I will not be your God.
耶和华说：“给他起名叫罗阿米（就是“非我民”的意思）；因为你们不作我的子民，我也不作你们的 神。


何1:10
Yet the number of the children of Israel shall be as the sand of the sea, which cannot be measured nor numbered; and it shall come to pass, that in the place where it was said them, Ye are not my people, there it shall be said unto them, Ye are the sons of the living God.
然而，以色列的人数必如海沙，不可量，不可数。从前在什么地方对他们说：'你们不是我的子民'，将来在那里必对他们说：'你们是永生 神的儿子。'


何1:11
Then shall the children of and the children of Israel be gathered together, and appoint themselves one head, and they shall come up out of the land: for great shall be the day of .
犹大人和以色列人必一同聚集，为自己立一个首领，从这地上去（或作“从被掳之地上来”），因为耶斯列的日子必为大日。
