斯10:1
And the king laid a tribute upon the land, and upon the the sea.
亚哈随鲁王使旱地和海岛的人民都进贡。


斯10:2
And all the acts of his power and of his might, and the declaration of the of , the king advanced him, are they not written in the book of of the kings of Media and ?
他以权柄能力所行的，并他抬举末底改使他高升的事，岂不都写在玛代和波斯王的历史上吗？


斯10:3
For the Jew was next king , and great among the Jews, and accepted of of his brethren, seeking the wealth of his people, and speaking peace to all his seed.
犹大人末底改作亚哈随鲁王的宰相，在犹大人中为大，得他众弟兄的喜悦，为本族的人求好处，向他们说和平的话。
