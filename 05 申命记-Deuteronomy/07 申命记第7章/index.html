When the LORD God shall bring into the land to possess it, and cast out many nations before , the , and the Girgashites, and the , and the , and the , and the , and the , seven nations greater and than ;

申7:2
And when the LORD God shall deliver them before ; them, and destroy them; make no them, nor them:
耶和华你 神将他们交给你击杀，那时你要把他们灭绝净尽，不可与他们立约，也不可怜恤他们；


申7:3
Neither make marriages with them; daughter not give his son, nor his daughter thou take son.
不可与他们结亲，不可将你的女儿嫁他们的儿子；也不可叫你的儿子娶他们的女儿，


申7:4
For they will turn away son from following me, that they may serve other gods: so will the anger of the LORD be against you, and destroy suddenly.
因为他必使你儿子转离不跟从主，去事奉别 神，以致耶和华的怒气向你们发作，就速速地将你们灭绝。


申7:5
But thus shall ye deal with them; ye shall destroy their , and break down their images, and cut down their , and burn their graven images with fire.
你们却要这样待他们：拆毁他们的祭坛，打碎他们的柱像，砍下他们的木偶，用火焚烧他们雕刻的偶像。


申7:6
For an holy people the LORD thy God: the LORD thy God chosen to be a special people himself, above all people that are upon the face of the earth.
因为你归耶和华你 神为圣洁的民，耶和华你 神从地上的万民中拣选你，特作自己的子民。


申7:7
The LORD did not set his love upon you, nor choose you, because ye were more in number than any people; for ye were of all people:
耶和华专爱你们，拣选你们，并非因你们的人数多于别民，原来你们的人数在万民中是最少的。


申7:8
But because the LORD loved you, and because he would keep the which he had sworn unto your fathers, the LORD brought you out with a hand, and you out of the house of , from the hand of king of Egypt.
只因耶和华爱你们，又因要守他向你们列祖所起的誓，就用大能的手领你们出来，从为奴之家救赎你们脱离埃及王法老的手。


申7:9
Know therefore that the LORD thy God, he is God, God, which and with them that love him and keep his to a thousand generations;
所以你要知道耶和华你的 神，他是 神，是信实的 神，向爱他、守他诫命的人守约施慈爱，直到千代；


申7:10
And them that hate him to their face, to destroy them: he will not be to him that him, he will him to his face.
向恨他的人当面报应他们，将他们灭绝。凡恨他的人，必报应他们，决不迟延。


申7:11
therefore keep the , and the statutes, and the judgments, which I command thee this day, to do them.
所以，你要谨守遵行我今日所吩咐你的诫命、律例、典章。


申7:12
it shall come to pass, if ye these judgments, and keep, and do them, that the LORD thy God shall keep unto thee the and the which he unto thy fathers:
你们果然听从这些典章，谨守遵行，耶和华你 神就必照他向你列祖所起的誓，守约施慈爱。


申7:13
And he will love thee, and thee, and thee: he will also the fruit of thy , and the fruit of thy land, thy corn, and thy wine, and oil, the increase of thy , and the of thy sheep, in the land which he unto thy fathers to give thee.
他必爱你，赐福与你，使你人数增多；也必在他向你列祖起誓应许给你的地上，赐福与你身所生的、地所产的，并你的五谷、新酒和油，以及牛犊、羊羔。


申7:14
be above all people: there shall not be male or female among you, or among your cattle.
你必蒙福胜过万民，你们的男女没有不能生养的，牲畜也没有不能生育的。


申7:15
And the LORD will take away from thee all , and will put none of diseases of Egypt, which thou , upon thee; but will lay them upon all them that hate thee.
耶和华必使一切的病症离开你，你所知道埃及各样的恶疾，他不加在你身上，只加在一切恨你的人身上。


申7:16
And thou shalt consume all the people which the LORD thy God shall deliver thee; eye shall have no pity upon them: neither shalt thou serve their gods; for that will be a unto thee.
耶和华你 神所要交给你的一切人民，你要将他们除灭，你眼不可顾惜他们。你也不可事奉他们的 神，因这必成为你的网罗。


申7:17
If thou shalt say in heart, These nations are more than I; how can I them?
你若心里说：'这些国的民比我更多，我怎能赶出他们呢？'


申7:18
Thou shalt not be afraid of them: but shalt well remember what the LORD thy God did unto , and unto all Egypt;
你不要惧怕他们，要牢牢记念耶和华你 神向法老和埃及全地所行的事，


申7:19
The great which eyes saw, and the signs, and the wonders, and the hand, and the stretched out arm, whereby the LORD thy God brought thee out: so shall the LORD thy God all the people of whom afraid.
就是你亲眼所看见的大试验、神迹、奇事和大能的手并伸出来的膀臂，都是耶和华你 神领你出来所用的。耶和华你 神必照样待你所惧怕的一切人民。


申7:20
Moreover the LORD thy God will send the among them, until they that are left, and hide themselves from thee, be destroyed.
并且耶和华你 神必打发黄蜂飞到他们中间，直到那剩下而藏躲的人从你面前灭亡。


申7:21
Thou shalt not be at them: for the LORD thy God is among you, a God and terrible.
你不要因他们惊恐，因为耶和华你 神在你们中间是大而可畏的 神。


申7:22
And the LORD thy God will put out those nations before thee by little and little: not consume them at once, of the field increase upon thee.
耶和华你 神必将这些国的民，从你面前渐渐赶出，你不可把他们速速灭尽，恐怕野地的兽多起来害你。


申7:23
But the LORD thy God shall deliver them unto thee, and shall destroy them with a destruction, until they be destroyed.
耶和华你 神必将他们交给你，大大地扰乱他们，直到他们灭绝了；


申7:24
And he shall deliver their kings into hand, and thou shalt destroy their name from under heaven: there shall no man be able to stand before thee, until thou have destroyed them.
又要将他们的君王交在你手中，你就使他们的名从天下消灭。必无一人能在你面前站立得住，直到你将他们灭绝了。


申7:25
images of their gods shall ye burn with fire: thou shalt not desire the silver or gold that is on them, nor take it unto thee, thou be : for it is an to the LORD thy God.
他们雕刻的神像，你们要用火焚烧，其上的金银你不可贪图，也不可收取，免得你因此陷入网罗，这原是耶和华你 神所憎恶的。


申7:26
Neither shalt thou bring an into thine house, thou be a thing like it: but thou shalt it, and thou shalt it; for it is a thing.
可憎的物，你不可带进家去，不然，你就成了当毁灭的，与那物一样。你要十分厌恶、十分憎嫌，因为这是当毁灭的物。
