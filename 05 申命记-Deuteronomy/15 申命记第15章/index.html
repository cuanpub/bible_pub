申15:1
At the end of every seven years make a release.
每逢七年末一年，你要施行豁免。


申15:2
And this is the manner of the release: Every creditor that ought his neighbour shall release it; he shall it of his neighbour, or of his brother; because it is called the LORD's release.
豁免的定例乃是这样：凡债主要把所借给邻舍的豁免了，不可向邻舍和弟兄追讨，因为耶和华的豁免年已经宣告了。


申15:3
Of a exact it again: but that which is with brother hand shall release;
若借给外邦人，你可以向他追讨；但借给你弟兄，无论是什么，你要松手豁免了。
