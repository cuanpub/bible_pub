申6:1
Now these are the , the statutes, and the judgments, which the LORD your God commanded to teach you, that ye might do them in the land ye go to possess it:
这是耶和华你们 神所吩咐教训你们的诫命、律例、典章，使你们在所要过去得为业的地上遵行，


申6:2
That fear the LORD God, to keep all his statutes and his , which I command , , and son, and son's son, all the days of life; and that days may be .
好叫你和你子子孙孙一生敬畏耶和华你的 神，谨守他的一切律例、诫命，就是我所吩咐你的，使你的日子得以长久。


申6:3
Hear therefore, O Israel, and observe to do it; that it may be well with , and that ye may increase , as the LORD God of thy fathers promised , in the land that with .
以色列啊！你要听，要谨守遵行，使你可以在那流奶与蜜之地得以享福，人数极其增多，正如耶和华你列祖的 神所应许你的。


申6:4
Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD:
以色列啊，你要听！耶和华我们 神是独一的主。


申6:5
And love the LORD thy God with all heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy might.
你要尽心、尽性、尽力爱耶和华你的 神。


申6:6
And these words, which I command this day, shall be in heart:
我今日所吩咐你的话都要记在心上，


申6:7
And teach them thy children, and talk of them when in house, and when thou by the way, and when thou down, and when thou up.
也要殷勤教训你的儿女，无论你坐在家里，行在路上，躺下，起来，都要谈论；


申6:8
And bind them for a sign upon hand, and they shall be as between eyes.
也要系在手上为记号，戴在额上为经文；


申6:9
And write them upon the posts of thy house, and on thy gates.
又要写在你房屋的门框上，并你的城门上。


申6:10
And it shall be, when the LORD thy God shall have brought into the land which he thy fathers, to , to , and to , to give thee great and cities, which thou buildedst not,
耶和华你的 神，领你进他向你列祖亚伯拉罕、以撒、雅各起誓应许给你的地。那里有城邑，又大又美，非你所建造的；


申6:11
And houses full of all good things, which thou filledst not, and wells digged, which thou diggedst not, and , which thou plantedst not; when have eaten and be full;
有房屋，装满各样美物，非你所装满的；有凿成的水井，非你所凿成的；还有葡萄园、橄榄园，非你所栽种的，你吃了而且饱足。


申6:12
Then thou forget the LORD, which brought thee forth out of the land of Egypt, from the house of .
那时，你要谨慎，免得你忘记将你从埃及地为奴之家领出来的耶和华。


申6:13
Thou shalt fear the LORD thy God, and serve him, and shalt his name.
你要敬畏耶和华你的 神，事奉他，指着他的名起誓。


申6:14
Ye shall not go after other gods, of the gods of the people which are round about you;
不可随从别 神，就是你们四围国民的 神，


申6:15
(For the LORD thy God is a God among you) the anger of the LORD thy God be against thee, and destroy thee from off the face of the earth.
因为在你们中间的耶和华你 神，是忌邪的 神。惟恐耶和华你 神的怒气向你发作，就把你从地上除灭。


申6:16
Ye shall not the LORD your God, as ye him in .
你们不可试探耶和华你们的 神，像你们在玛撒那样试探他。


申6:17
Ye shall keep the of the LORD your God, and his testimonies, and his statutes, which he commanded thee.
要留意遵守耶和华你们 神所吩咐的诫命、法度、律例。


申6:18
And thou shalt do that which is right and good in the sight of the LORD: that it may be well with thee, and that go in and possess the good land which the LORD thy fathers,
耶和华眼中看为正看为善的，你都要遵行，使你可以享福，并可以进去得耶和华向你列祖起誓应许的那美地，


申6:19
To cast out all thine enemies from before thee, as the LORD spoken.
照耶和华所说的，从你面前撵出你的一切仇敌。


申6:20
And when thy son thee in time to come, saying, What mean the testimonies, and the statutes, and the judgments, which the LORD our God commanded you?
日后，你的儿子问你说：'耶和华我们 神吩咐你们的这些法度、律例、典章是什么意思呢？'


申6:21
Then thou shalt say thy son, We were Pharaoh's in Egypt; and the LORD brought us out of Egypt with a hand:
你就告诉你的儿子说：'我们在埃及作过法老的奴仆，耶和华用大能的手将我们从埃及领出来，


申6:22
And the LORD signs and wonders, great and , upon Egypt, upon , and upon all his household, before our eyes:
在我们眼前，将重大可怕的神迹奇事，施行在埃及地和法老并他全家的身上，


申6:23
And he brought us out , that he might bring us in, to give us the land which he our fathers.
将我们从那里领出来，要领我们进入他向我们列祖起誓应许之地，把这地赐给我们。


申6:24
And the LORD commanded us to do all these statutes, to fear the LORD our God, for our good always, that he might alive, as it is at this day.
耶和华又吩咐我们遵行这一切律例，要敬畏耶和华我们的 神，使我们常得好处，蒙他保全我们的生命，像今日一样。


申6:25

我们若照耶和华我们 神所吩咐的一切诫命，谨守遵行，这就是我们的义了。'
