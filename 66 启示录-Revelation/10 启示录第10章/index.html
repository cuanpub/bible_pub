启10:1
And I saw another angel come down from heaven, a cloud: and a was upon his head, and his face was as it were the sun, and his feet as :
我又看见另有一位大力的天使，从天降下，披着云彩，头上有虹。脸面像日头，两脚像火柱。


启10:2
And he had in his hand a little book open: and he set his right foot upon the sea, and his left foot on the earth,
他手里拿着小书卷，是展开的。他右脚踏海，左脚踏地，


启10:3
And cried with a loud voice, as when a lion : and when he had cried, seven their voices.
大声呼喊，好像狮子吼叫。呼喊完了，就有七雷发声。


启10:4
And when the seven had their voices, I was about to write: and I heard a voice from heaven saying me, those things which the seven , and write them not.
七雷发声之后，我正要写出来，就听见从天上有声音说：“七雷所说的你要封上，不可写出来。”


启10:5
And the angel which I saw stand upon the sea and upon the earth lifted up his hand ,
我所看见的那踏海踏地的天使向天举起右手来，


启10:6
And by him that for ever and ever, who created heaven, and the things that are, and the earth, and the things that are, and the sea, and the things which are , that there should be time no longer:
指着那创造天和天上之物，地和地上之物，海和海中之物，直活到永永远远的，起誓说：“不再有时日了（或作“不再耽延了”）！”


启10:7
But in the days of the voice of the angel, when he shall begin to sound, the mystery of God should be finished, as he declared to his servants .
但在第七位天使吹号发声的时候， 神的奥秘就成全了，正如 神所传给他仆人众先知的佳音。


启10:8
And the voice which I heard from heaven me again, and said, Go and take the little book which is open in the hand of the angel which upon the sea and upon the earth.
我先前从天上所听见的那声音又吩咐我说：“你去，把那踏海踏地之天使手中展开的小书卷取过来。”


启10:9
And I went the angel, and said him, Give me the little book. And he said me, Take it, and eat it up; and it shall make belly bitter, but it shall be in mouth sweet as honey.
我就走到天使那里，对他说：“请你把小书卷给我。”他对我说：“你拿着吃尽了，便叫你肚子发苦，然而在你口中要甜如蜜。”


启10:10
And I took the little book out of the angel's hand, and ate it up; and it was in my mouth sweet as honey: and as soon as I had eaten it, my belly was bitter.
我从天使手中把小书卷接过来，吃尽了，在我口中果然甜如蜜，吃了以后，肚子觉得发苦了。


启10:11
And he said unto me, must again before many peoples, and nations, and tongues, and kings.
天使（原文作“他们”）对我说：“你必指着多民、多国、多方、多王再说预言。”
